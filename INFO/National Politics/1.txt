Việt Nam, France hold defence strategy and cooperation dialogue

he two heads of delegations agreed that since the second dialogue in April 2022, bilateral defence cooperation has been continuously consolidated and strengthened in a practical, effective direction consistent with signed cooperation documents and agreements, especially the Joint Vision Statement on Việt Nam - France defence cooperation for the 2018 – 2028 period and the Framework Agreement on Việt Nam - France defence cooperation.
PARIS — Deputy Minister of Defence Sen. Lieut. Gen. Hoàng Xuân Chiến and Director General for International Relations and Strategy at the French Ministry of the Armed Forces Alice Rufo co-chaired the third Việt Nam-France defence strategy and cooperation dialogue in Paris on December 18 (local time).
